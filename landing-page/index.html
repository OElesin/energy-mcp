Three tools, one endpoint

Everything your agent needs for energy-aware decisions.

📊

get_spot_prices

Current and upcoming hourly EPEX Spot electricity prices for Germany.

🔮

get_price_forecast

Tomorrow's day-ahead hourly prices. Published daily at 14:00 CET.

📈

get_price_stats

Average, min, max, cheapest and most expensive hours.

⚡

find_cheapest_window

Find the optimal time to run an appliance — EV charging, dishwasher, heat pump.

⚖️

compare_tariffs

Dynamic vs fixed tariff simulation for a German household.

💰

calculate_energy_cost

What X kWh costs at current spot prices, with week-over-week comparison.

📉

analyze_price_trends

Weekly averages, volatility, daily breakdowns, and trend direction.

Data source: EPEX Spot via aWATTar • Updated hourly at 14:00 CET with next-day prices
